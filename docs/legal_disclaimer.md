# Legal disclaimer

Public Accountability Radar is a public-interest research and accountability tool.

- It does **not** determine or allege corruption, fraud, criminal conduct, or misconduct.
- It presents anomaly and risk signals that may indicate possible control weaknesses or spending patterns worth scrutiny.
- All outputs should be independently reviewed by qualified humans and interpreted with context.
- Source evidence, confidence scores, and alternative explanations are provided to reduce misinterpretation and defamation risk.
